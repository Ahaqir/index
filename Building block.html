NBA 2018

The Golden State Warriors are going to win the NBA Championship for the third straight year.

LeBron James signed with the Los Angeles Lakers in the summer but his team is still not good enough to beat the Warriors

The Houston Rockets acquired Carmelo Anthony in the summer but had to give up Trevor Ariza, one of their best defenders.

In the. Eastern Conference, the Boston Celtics are favorites to make it to the. NBA Finals and lose to the Warriors.
